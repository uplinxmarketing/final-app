# Analytics Reporting Skill

## Key Metrics
- ROAS: good > 2.0, excellent > 4.0, pause < 1.0
- CPM: good < €8, high > €15 (investigate)
- CTR: good > 1.5%, low < 0.8% (review creative)
- CPC: depends on market, flag if > €2

## Report Format
Always include:
1. Summary (3 bullet points max)
2. Top performing ad with reason
3. Underperforming ads with recommendation
4. Suggested next actions

## Recommendations
- ROAS < 1.0 for 3+ days: suggest pause
- CTR dropping: suggest creative refresh
- CPM rising: suggest audience or placement review

## Anomaly Flags
- Flag any ad with 0 impressions after 24 hours
- Flag any campaign spending 2x daily budget
- Flag any disapproved ads immediately
