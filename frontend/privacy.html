Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Uplinx Post ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our scheduling and publishing tool for Facebook and Instagram. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

1 What the App Does

Uplinx Post is a scheduling tool that lets you create, schedule, and publish posts to your own Facebook Pages and Instagram Business or Creator accounts. The app interacts with Meta's official APIs (Facebook Graph API and Instagram Graph API) on your behalf, only after you explicitly connect your accounts.

2 Information We Collect

When you connect your Facebook or Instagram account, the app may access and store the following information through Meta's APIs:

  • Your name, profile picture, and Facebook/Instagram user ID
  • A list of Facebook Pages and Instagram accounts you manage
  • Access tokens issued by Meta that allow the app to publish on your behalf
  • The content (text, images, videos, captions, hashtags) of posts you choose to schedule through the app
  • Scheduling metadata such as the date and time you want a post published

We do not collect or store passwords. Authentication is handled entirely by Meta through OAuth.

We may also collect basic technical information such as your email address (if you create an account), login timestamps, and error logs needed to keep the service running.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information only to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session
  • Display your connected Pages and accounts inside the app
  • Publish the posts you schedule, at the times you choose
  • Provide customer support and troubleshoot issues
  • Improve the reliability and security of the service

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside the scheduling and publishing features you actively use.

4 Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your data is shared only with Meta Platforms (Facebook and Instagram) as required to publish the content you have scheduled. We may also share data with service providers (such as hosting and database providers) strictly for the purpose of operating the app, and only under confidentiality obligations.

We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users.

5 Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers and protected by industry-standard measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and access controls. Access tokens are stored encrypted. While no online service can guarantee absolute security, we work to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6 Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You can request deletion at any time (see Section 7).

When you disconnect your Facebook or Instagram account from the app, the associated access tokens are deleted within 30 days.

7 Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time by disconnecting your accounts inside the app or by revoking the app's access from your Facebook settings: facebook.com/settings → Business Tools

To request data deletion, email uplinxmarketing@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will confirm and complete the deletion within 30 days.

8 Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 (or under 16 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or by email.
